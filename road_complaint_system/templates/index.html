How It Works

Simple 4-step process to report road issues

1
Scan QR Code

Find the QR code on any road signboard or pole and scan it with your phone camera.

2
Describe Problem

Select the issue type and describe the problem. Road info is auto-detected.

3
Upload Photos

Take and upload photos of the damage. A picture is worth a thousand words.

4
Track & Resolve

Get a complaint ID. Track status online. MLA and engineers are notified.
